Market Solutions

Industries we serve

Our products and expertise span a wide array of markets, enabling customers to innovate and compete with confidence.

01

Flexible Packaging

Films, pouches, and wrappers—colour and additives that perform in high-speed converting environments.

02

Rigid Packaging

Bottles, containers, and closures requiring precise colour consistency and regulatory compliance.

03

Consumer Products

Housewares, appliances, and lifestyle goods where aesthetics and durability go hand in hand.

Conduit & Industrial
